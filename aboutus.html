About Us

OBJECTIVES OF J&K YOUTH WELFARE DEVELOPMENT SOCIETY :

The main objectives to be pursued by the society are:
� The society will work towards Development of youth, so as to render them ethically, morally and economically productive and socially useful. Character building of today youth shall remain area of our concern, during the campaigning.
� To campaign invariably about the necessity and need of proper education, in order to create mass awareness about mass literacy, with emphasis to develop a mechanism to educate drop outs, who left their studies, due to unavoidable circumstances.
� Besides promotion of education among the youth of J&k, society would work in the area of counseling /coordination in the matter of higher education and employment wherever feasible
� Promotion of science, literature, art and cultural development , shall remain the priority. Besides attention shall remain focused on to work towards building a just society, aiming to wage a battle against discrimination based on class, creed, exploitation sexual crime & violence against women shall also remain main area of our concern.
� To work towards overall welfare and Development of youth comprising upon both male, female, weak /neglected, Educated/illiterate, rural /Urbanized. as well as handicapped ones.
� To create mass awareness at the Gross root levels on various welfare, upliftment /betterment schemes and programmes run by the state and Central Government , for the welfare of the youth and other sections of the society.
� To guide and help young artisans and craftsman, in improving their skills, for starting their own gainful Enterprises. Guidance and information service shall also be made available to them about marketing of their works /crafts.
� To work towards comprehensive health Education, awareness eradication of social evils viz Aids, exploitation, Drug addiction, dowry etc
� For channelizing their energies, all round growth and development , sports and cultural interaction /activities shall be encouraged. This activity shall help in identifying the hidden local talents , as well.
